Techqueria exists as a safe space for Latinx professionals in tech to feel included, thrive and elevate each other.

However, if you do not identify as Latinx, you will still receive an invitation to our Slack community as a multi-channel guest with access to #announcements, #jobs, #events and #allies where you are welcome to share resources, events or job postings.

We are firm in this policy of not allowing allies to get additional access to channels beyond the ones outlined above.

With your support, Techqueria can become a stronger & more valuable community for Latinx in Tech.
